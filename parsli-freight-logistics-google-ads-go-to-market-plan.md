**Prepared:** March 17, 2026

**Target:** Freight & Logistics companies in Chicago, IL
**Goal:** Acquire first paying customers via Google Ads

## 1. Executive Summary

After analyzing 83+ keywords across 8 industry verticals and 3 US cities using Google Keyword Planner, web research, and competitor analysis, we recommend Parsli launch its first Google Ads campaign targeting **freight and logistics companies in Chicago, Illinois**.

This plan projects **4-5 paying customers per month** at a **3.2x first-month ROI** — acquiring customers at ~$222 each against an estimated LTV of $708+ per customer.

### 2.1b Vertical Elimination Detail

**Eliminated — No viable search demand:**

- **Car Dealerships:** 4 of 5 keywords returned zero search volume. The one keyword with data ("car dealership document management," 50/mo) shows -100% 3-month trend. Auto dealers are not searching for document extraction software.
- **Insurance:** Only 1 of 4 keywords has data (50/mo). No CPC data means zero advertiser interest. The market doesn't exist at the SMB level.
- **Healthcare:** Only 1 of 3 keywords has data (50/mo). HIPAA compliance would also add significant product complexity for minimal search demand.

**Eliminated — Poor unit economics for $1K budget:**

- **Real Estate:** "Lease abstraction software" has 500/mo volume but CPCs of **$17.49–$89.28** — the most expensive across all verticals. $1K budget yields only 11-57 clicks. Additionally, Parseur already dominates this vertical with ready-made Zillow/Realtor/Trulia templates.
- **Legal:** "Legal document automation" has 500/mo volume but is **declining -90% YoY**. CPCs of $16.00–$59.74 limit $1K budget to 17-62 clicks. Established competitors (Ironclad, DocuSign CLM, Juro) dominate.
- **Accounting:** All 3 keywords with data are at 50/mo — total volume of only 150/mo. Crowded with Bill.com, Dext, Hubdoc, AutoEntry. No room to differentiate.

**Winner — Freight/Logistics:**

Freight has **3.4x more keyword volume** than the next-best vertical (1,700+ vs 550), **7x more keywords with search data** (7+ vs 1-2), the **cheapest CPCs** ($5.58 vs $16-17), and is the **only vertical with surging demand** (+900%, +∞). With $1K budget, freight delivers ~280 clicks vs 11-83 for other verticals.

## 9. Roadmap & Tasks

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

- [ ] **Create freight-specific landing page** at parsli.co/freight
  - Hero section with freight invoice extraction visual
  - Pain points section (2-4 hrs/day manual entry, $15.96/invoice manual cost)
  - Document types section (BOL, freight invoice, packing list, commercial invoice)
  - ROI calculator
  - CTA: "Start Free — 30 Pages/Month"
- [ ] **Build 2-3 pre-made freight parser templates** in the product
  - Freight invoice template (carrier name, charges, weight, origin, destination)
  - Bill of lading template (shipper, consignee, commodity, weight, container #)
  - Commercial invoice template (seller, buyer, items, quantities, values)
- [ ] **Set up Google Ads account** for freight campaign
  - Create campaign targeting Chicago DMA
  - Set up 4 ad groups (Freight Core, Freight Software, Document Extraction, Invoice Specific)
  - Write 3 ad variations per ad group
  - Add negative keyword list
  - Set daily budget to $33/day ($1,000/mo)
  - Enable conversion tracking (sign_up_completed event)

### Phase 2: Launch & Optimize (Week 3-4)

- [ ] **Launch Google Ads campaign** (Chicago, freight keywords)
- [ ] **Monitor daily for first 7 days:**
  - Check search terms report — add irrelevant terms as negatives
  - Monitor CPC vs. estimates — adjust bids if needed
  - Verify conversion tracking is firing correctly
- [ ] **Set up weekly reporting dashboard:**
  - Clicks, impressions, CTR, CPC by ad group
  - Signups, free-to-paid conversions
  - Cost per acquisition (CPA)

### Phase 3: Iterate (Week 5-8)

- [ ] **A/B test landing page** (headline, CTA, social proof variations)
- [ ] **Expand keyword list** based on Search Terms Report
  - Add high-performing search terms as exact match keywords
  - Identify new long-tail opportunities
- [ ] **Adjust bid strategy** based on conversion data
  - Shift budget toward highest-converting ad groups
  - Pause underperforming keywords
- [ ] **Create retargeting campaign** ($100/mo from main budget)
  - Target visitors who viewed landing page but didn't sign up

### Phase 4: Scale Decision (Month 3)

- [ ] **Evaluate results against projections:**
  - Are we hitting ~5 paying customers/month?
  - Is CPA below $218?
  - Is free-to-paid rate above 35%?
- [ ] **If hitting targets → Scale:**
  - Increase budget to $2,000/mo
  - Expand to Dallas-Fort Worth (2nd freight hub)
  - Add freight-specific SEO content (blog posts, guides)
- [ ] **If underperforming → Pivot:**
  - Test horizontal keywords only (automate data entry, document parser)
  - Test different city (Dallas or Atlanta)
  - Revisit landing page conversion optimization

### Phase 5: Expand (Month 4-6)

- [ ] **Geographic expansion:** Add Dallas-Fort Worth, then Atlanta
- [ ] **Content marketing:** Publish freight-specific SEO content
  - "How to Automate Freight Invoice Processing"
  - "Bill of Lading Data Extraction Guide"
  - "Best Freight Audit Software for Small Brokers"
- [ ] **Product development:** Build freight-specific features based on customer feedback
  - Pre-trained models for common freight document layouts
  - CargoWise / TMS integration
  - Freight-specific field types (weight, container numbers, HS codes)

### C.4 Decision: Freight Remains the Best $1K Vertical

**Freight wins for $1K/month because:**

- CPCs too expensive for $1K budget
