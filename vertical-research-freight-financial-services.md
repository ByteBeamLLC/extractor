### 3.4 Buyer Persona Quality

**Freight:**
- 3PL operations managers processing 300+ documents/day
- Freight brokers reconciling carrier invoices
- Warehouse clerks entering BOL data into WMS
- **Decision-maker with budget authority, clear pain point, immediate need**

**Financial Services — Bank Statements:**
- Bookkeepers converting statements for reconciliation
- Loan officers verifying income from applicant statements
- Small business owners doing DIY accounting
- Freelance data entry workers (not buyers)
- **Mixed intent — many are consumers, not B2B buyers**

**Financial Services — AP/Accounting:**
- AP managers processing vendor invoices
- Controllers automating month-end close
- CFOs evaluating AP automation platforms
- **High-quality buyers but already shopping enterprise tools (Bill.com, Tipalti)**
